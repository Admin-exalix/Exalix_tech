From Clay to Cloud

The Digital Transformation Journey of Nirali Ceramic with ERPNext

An ERPNext Success Story by Exalix Tech

The Client: Nirali Ceramic

A powerhouse in the tile industry, Nirali Ceramic operates on a massive scale. With a state-of-the-art production unit in Morbi and headquarters in Ahmedabad, they are a name synonymous with quality and volume.

  • Production: 12,000+ Boxes Per Day
  • Infrastructure: 9 Warehouses & a 4-Tier Supply Chain
  • Market: 68 Domestic & 4 International Clients

The Tipping Point

Growth is a double-edged sword. For Nirali Ceramic, their success was creating crippling operational challenges. Their existing, fragmented systems couldn't keep up, leading to critical bottlenecks.

  • Data Chaos: Critical information was siloed in spreadsheets and legacy software, making a unified business view impossible.
  • Inventory Nightmare: With 9 warehouses, tracking stock in real-time was a guessing game, leading to costly overstocking and missed sales opportunities.
  • Production Delays: Lack of integration between sales and production meant planning was reactive, causing delays and inefficiencies on the factory floor.
  • Financial Blind Spots: The accounting team struggled to close books on time, and management lacked the real-time financial data needed for strategic decisions.

The Implementation Journey

Exalix Tech orchestrated a meticulous, phased implementation of ERPNext to ensure a seamless transition without disrupting Nirali's high-volume operations.

Phase 1:Discovery & Blueprinting

Deep dive into Nirali's unique workflows. Mapped every process from procurement to final delivery, creating a strategic blueprint for the ERP system.

Phase 2:Configuration & Core Setup

Implemented core modules: Accounting, Buying, and Inventory. Digitized all 9 warehouses, establishing a single source of truth for finance and stock.

Phase 3:Manufacturing & QC Integration

Rolled out the Manufacturing and Quality Control modules. Configured complex Bills of Material (BOMs) and established digital quality checkpoints.

Phase 4:Go-Live & Training

Launched the full system, including the Selling module. Conducted extensive, role-based training for all staff, ensuring high user adoption and a successful launch.
